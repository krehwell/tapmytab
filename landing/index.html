what's in the box

(spoiler: quite a lot, for 2 megabytes)

drag. drop. done. kanban

boards, columns, cards, as many as you want. shuffle cards across columns, hop them between boards, honestly just drag everything everywhere. your other tabs keep up instantly, no refresh needed.

actual screenshot, actual pixels ↑

too messy for words? draw it sketch

every card has an excalidraw canvas built in. sometimes the idea is a picture, not a sentence. scribble it down before it escapes, text is optional.

write your way markdown

full markdown and rich text on every card. checklists, headings, links, images, highlights. paste your brain in whatever shape it shows up.

find it before you forget why search

fuzzy search across every board and card, typos welcome. tag cards with your own labels, filter what matters, slap a due date on it so the deadline stays loud.
